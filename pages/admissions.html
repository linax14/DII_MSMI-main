FAQ's

1. How can I apply for the Master's in Interactive Systems and Media?

You can apply online through our application portal. Make sure to follow all the instructions provided in the application process.

2. What are the admission requirements for the program?

Admission requirements include a bachelor's degree in a related field, submission of academic documents such as transcripts and letters of motivation.

3. What documents are required for the application?

Typical required documents include academic certificates, citizen documentation, a resume, letters of motivation, and possibly a portfolio of previous work related to interactive systems and media.

4. Is there a deadline for submitting the application?

Yes, it is crucial to adhere to the application deadlines. They are stated on this website.

5. Is prior experience in programming or design necessary for admission to the Master's in Interactive Systems and Media?

While prior experience is valued, it is not mandatory. The program is designed for students with diverse backgrounds. We provide opportunities to develop technical skills during the course.
