WELCOME TO ESMAD

The School of Media Arts and Design (ESMAD) at Polytechnic of Porto (P.PORTO) positions itself as a national and international benchmark in training competitive, creative, and innovative professionals in the fields of Cinema, Design, Photography, Multimedia, and Web. As a cross-disciplinary project in the realms of Media Arts and Design, it aims to provide highly qualified professionals, foster the development of systematic skills, and instill artistic awareness in the following domains: audiovisual, photography, cinema, graphic design, industrial design, multimedia, digital games, and web technologies. We blend strong technical competencies with an artistic vocation of an authorial profile. With a lab-oriented educational approach strongly focused on employability, we invest in training environments conducive to innovation, creativity, and excellence. We promote multidisciplinarity, collaboration, and teamwork. Located on Campus 2 of Polytechnic of Porto, our facilities boast quality spaces and excellent equipment.

ABOUT INTERACTIVE SYSTEMS AND MEDIA

The Master's program combines a strong technological component with a high artistic capacity in the process of conceiving and creating products, experiences, and systems with varying degrees of interactivity. This program aims to address and explore the opportunities provided by new technologies and digital media. The Master's degree aims to equip students with theoretical and practical knowledge applied to web development and digital platform design, the creation of interactive environments for virtual/augmented reality, video games, or interactive installations, as well as the production of digital animation and motion graphics for interactive contexts, videomapping, or performances.
