Interface and Interaction Design

objectives

  • Explore the history, applications, and technologies of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).
  • Foster creativity and innovation in designing and prototyping interfaces and interaction models, emphasizing both graphic and natural dimensions.
  • Apply diverse navigation, content architecture (wireframing), and interaction techniques in Web User Interfaces (WUIs).
  • Utilize computer vision and physical computing techniques to enhance interface design.

Embark on a journey where creativity meets technology, transforming your ability to craft engaging and innovative digital experiences.

Join us in shaping a future where sound and image converge to create immersive experiences.
